## Hello, World

I'm Shaeel Afsar — a Lead Software Engineer based in Chicago with over 10 years of experience spanning software engineering and customer-facing roles at companies like Amazon, United Airlines, and UnitedHealth Group.

This site is a place to share my professional journey, showcase projects I've worked on, and connect with people who are building interesting things.

## What I bring to the table

My career path is a bit unconventional. I started in sales and customer engagement at Sprint and Comcast, where I learned the art of consultative selling, needs assessment, and clear communication. That foundation proved invaluable when I transitioned into software engineering.

Today, I focus on:

- **Scalable backend architectures** using Java, Spring Boot, and AWS
- **Solution design and technical demos** that bridge engineering and business
- **Team mentorship** and stakeholder communication
- **Cloud-native development** with microservices, Kubernetes, and CI/CD

## What's next

I'll be updating this site over time with more projects, thoughts on engineering leadership, and lessons learned from building software at scale. Stay tuned.
