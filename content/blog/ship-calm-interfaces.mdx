## The tension

Product teams move quickly, and quick movement often creates noisy interfaces. Extra treatments, extra states, and extra controls stack up until the screen starts explaining the team instead of helping the user.

## The practice

The antidote is rarely minimalism for its own sake. It is usually stronger hierarchy, tighter component rules, and a willingness to remove cleverness that does not improve the task.

## What changes

Calm interfaces feel more trustworthy because they direct attention carefully. That trust is a product feature.
