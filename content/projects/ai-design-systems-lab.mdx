## Overview

AI Design Systems Lab imagines a product experience where design tokens, docs, and UI previews stay in sync while teams experiment quickly.

### Scope

- Built a modular Next.js marketing shell with editorial pacing
- Modeled token governance and component documentation flows
- Balanced cinematic visuals with clear technical storytelling

### Outcome

The concept shows how automation can speed up collaboration without weakening the consistency of the interface language.
