## Overview

This project modernized an editorial web experience with clearer templates, cleaner content hierarchy, and a stronger component foundation.

### Key work

- Rebuilt high-traffic article templates in Next.js
- Introduced reusable content blocks and metadata patterns
- Improved accessibility and dark-mode consistency across long-form pages

### Outcome

The new system made publishing faster for authors while giving readers a calmer, more legible reading experience.
