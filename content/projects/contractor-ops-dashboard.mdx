## Overview

A product team needed a dependable internal dashboard for tracking field work, blockers, and weekly delivery targets.

### Scope

- Built task views, status filters, and summary reporting interfaces
- Connected frontend workflows to internal APIs and role-based actions
- Added clearer empty states, alerts, and mobile-safe layouts

### Outcome

Operators spent less time interpreting status tables and more time resolving issues quickly.
